LinkedIn Figma Course Project | Designed Using Figma

Overview

In this course, I have learned all the skills necessary to migrate into Figma. We have created a design project from scratch in order for us to review all the features of Figma and to call out places where Figma will be different than what you're used to. After completing this course, I can create style sheets, build a prototype that uses smart animations, understand the robustness of frames and collaborate with stakeholders, designers and your development team.
